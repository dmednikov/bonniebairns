Our Philosophy

At Bonnie Bairns Childcare Services we strive to provide a loving, stimulating, nurturing and healthy environment where your child will feel safe and secure. An environment where children learn to feel good about themselves and their achievements and where they are respected as individuals.

Our program is geared to enhance the intellectual, social, emotional, physical and creative development of your child and one which allows children to develop at their own individual pace.
